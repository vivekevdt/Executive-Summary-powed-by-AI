### üéØ 2. Project Vision & Executive Summary
The **SPE Executive Summary Generator** is a mission-critical AI application designed for **Zuari Industries**. It automates the extraction and comparison of weekly operational data from complex PowerPoint decks, generating decision-ready executive reviews in a standardized, leadership-safe format.

By leveraging **GPT-4o-mini** intelligence, the system identifies performance deltas, equipment efficiencies, and supply chain bottlenecks, presenting them in a professional Word document with zero human calculation error.

---

### üìà 3. Proposed Standardized Weekly Executive Summary Format
*The following structure defines the high-fidelity data and narratives generated by the system.*

#### **1. Cover Page**
The system automatically generates a professional title page featuring the **Zuari / Adventz** corporate identity.
*   **Context**: Identifies the specific Sugar Mill and the comparative week range.
*   **Audit Trail**: Includes the precise date of generation and a unique session ID.

#### **2. Executive Overview**
*   **2.1 Management Snapshot**: A one-page view of wins, concerns, and overall "Traffic-Light" status indicators.
*   **2.2 Dashboard Table**: A high-level visual summary of Crushing, Recovery, Power, and Distillery WoW trends.

#### **3. Operational Performance Review**
*   **Analysis**: Compares actual crushing against installed capacity and WoW averages.
*   **Table 3.1**: Quantitative summary of Crushed MT and Extraction percentages.

#### **4. Recovery & Process Efficiency**
*   **Insights**: Deep dive into Boiling House Losses and Pol Balance.
*   **Focus**: Identifies "Unknown Losses" to drive engineering accountability.

#### **5. Sugar Production & Quality**
*   **Trends**: Monitors Grade-wise output (S-30, M-31) and ICUMSA quality benchmarks.

#### **6. Power & Utilities Performance**
*   **KPIs**: Tracks Steam Consumption, Boiler availability, and Grid Export metrics.
*   **Table 4.1**: Auxiliary Consumption vs. Grid Export breakdown.

#### **7. Distillery Performance**
*   **Metrics**: Ethanol yield per ton of molasses and fermentation efficiency.
*   **Thresholds**: Alerts if yields fall below the 270 L/MT benchmark.

#### **8. Cane Planning & Supply Chain**
*   **Status**: Evaluates Indent vs. Actual arrival.
*   **Risk**: Highlights "Fresh vs. Stale" cane ratios to predict recovery impacts.

#### **9. Engineering & Maintenance Review**
*   **Downtime**: Categorizes hours into Planned vs. Unplanned.
*   **Action**: Tracks the resolution speed of critical milling tandems.

#### **10. Quality, EHS & Compliance**
*   **Safety**: Reports Zero-Harm culture metrics including "Near Miss" reporting and audit compliance status.

#### **11. Capital Projects (CAPEX) Tracker**
*   **Status**: Visual tracker for ongoing investments like Boiler automation or Evaporator upgrades.

#### **12. Risks & Watch-outs**
*   **Matrix**: Impact vs. Likelihood analysis for imminent operational or weather disruptions.

#### **13. Action Plan & Management Focus**
*   **Ownership**: Clear assignment of tasks to HODs based on AI-identified gaps.
